<div style="padding: 0px 0px 20px;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">The North West Healthy Living (HL) Clubs have been keeping our residents active through a variety of low to high-intensity workouts. Joining the HL Clubs has enabled many members young and old to make lifelong friends and reap the health benefits of staying active.</p>   
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">
From the first HL Club that was launched in 2002 to the formation of new clubs over the years, the Clubs have become more than just a fitness regime. The members have become family, bringing people from all walks of life together and forging strong bonds within the community. 
</p>
</div>

<div style="padding: 0px 0px 20px;">
<h2 style="color: #D5262B; font-weight: bold; font-size: x-large;">
  <strong>Discover our healthy living clubs</strong>
</h2>

<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">All you need is a pair of sports shoes, sportswear and you are all set to join any of the 6 HL Clubs!
</p>
</div>

<div style="text-align: center; padding: 0px 15px;">
  <img src="/images/clubs.jpg" style="width: 90%; max-width: 100%; margin: 0px auto;">
</div>

<div style="padding: 20px 0px; word-spacing: -1px; word-break: keep-all;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">If you are looking for high-intensity workouts, try the <strong>North West FitnessX Club</strong>. Launched in 2021, the club offers four high-intensity workouts such as CrossFit, Fight-Do, KPOP Fitness and Zumba, for fitness junkies who are down for some cardio training with professional trainers.</p> 

<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">Catered for those who enjoy running outdoors, the <strong>North West Running Club</strong> builds your stamina and lets you bond as you run together with other like-minded individuals. All these while exploring the North West on foot.</p> 

<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">Try out <strong>North West Qigong</strong> and <strong>North West Tai Chi Clubs</strong> if you prefer low-intensity exercises. Practising Qigong and Tai Chi regularly are known to keep your health in good order and promote active ageing lifestyle.</p> 

<p style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 26px;">Through the years, many clubs have grown into a true health and wellness community. The <strong>North West Dance-Fit Club</strong> and <strong>North West Brisk Walking Club</strong> recently celebrated their 15th and 20th anniversary respectively, bringing together their members to take part in mass activities and reminisce as they take a trip down memory&nbsp;lane.</p>
</div>
